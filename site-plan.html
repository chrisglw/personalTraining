Overview

Purpose

This is my personal training website for people who want to change their body, and reach its best potential to feel and look healthier. I offer personalized training programs and work closely with you on a one-on-one basis to help you achieve your fitness goals and become the best version of YOURSELF!

I'll assist you in setting personal goals and offer tailored guidance that aligns with your needs, schedule, and progress, enabling you to achieve your fitness objectives.

Audience

This website is aimed at individuals worldwide who have internet and gym access. They should be committed to transforming their bodies to achieve their optimum potential through personalized online programs.

Residents of the Rexburg, Idaho area have the option to book one-on-one, in-person sessions.

I require all participants to sign a waiver acknowledging that I am not liable for any injuries that may arise from the use of my programs. Therefore, I only work with individuals who are 18 years of age or older.

Home page

No more aimless Instagram workouts. No more starving to a restrictive diet. I will design a fully customized workout and nutrition plan based on the most effective routine for you.

I will check in with you regularly to review your progress, keep you motivated, make adjustments to your plan, and help you stay on track to reach your goals.

Images for the Home page

Hi, my name is Christian Landaverde. I am an online and in person fitness coach and trainer.

I began my fitness journey during my teenage years in high school because I was really skinny. I got a gym membership at the age of 18 when I graduated high school, and as time went on, I started to have a passion for intense weight training.

Since then, I have always enjoyed the process of physically and mentally changing for the better. I believe everyone is capable of becoming the best version of themselves, so I want to spread inspiration and motivation to many, to show them that it is possible!

I did not have a trainer when I started, so I spent a lot of time watching YouTube videos. I have had many injuries in the process, so I have spent the last 5 years researching fitness, and better ways to work out and eat. I also took some nutrition, and fitness classes in College.

Let me help you save you all that time, and injuries by sharing my knowledge with you, so that you can start your fitness journey.

Programs

Online

My online program is designed so that you can work out from anywhere in the world whether you decide to work at home or at the gym.

Once you sign up, I am going to schedule a zoom call with you, so I can design your fully customized workout and nutrition plan.

Go ahead and sign up now, so that we can start with your transformation to become the best version of yourself!

In Person

Have you thought about going to the gym, but you feel intimidated because you do not really know what you are doing?

Do not worry! You can schedule an in person session with me, and I will be next to you during the whole workout session. I will be personally showing you how to execute the exercises step by step, counting your repetitions, and checking the rest time.

I'll be in touch with you within 2 - 3 business days to schedule an 1 on 1 in person training session.
