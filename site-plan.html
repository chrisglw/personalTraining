Overview

Purpose

This is my personal training website for people who want to change their body, and reach its best potential to feel and look healthier. I offer personalized training programs and work closely with you on a one-on-one basis to help you achieve your fitness goals and become the best version of YOURSELF!

I'll assist you in setting personal goals and offer tailored guidance that aligns with your needs, schedule, and progress, enabling you to achieve your fitness objectives.

Audience

This website is aimed at individuals worldwide who have internet and gym access. They should be committed to transforming their bodies to achieve their optimum potential through personalized online programs.

Residents of the Rexburg, Idaho area have the option to book one-on-one, in-person sessions.

I require all participants to sign a waiver acknowledging that I am not liable for any injuries that may arise from the use of my programs. Therefore, I only work with individuals who are 18 years of age or older.

Hi, my name is Christian. I am an online and in person fitness trainer.

I began my fitness journey during my teenage years when I felt dissatisfied with my physique. Upon graduating high school, I eagerly stepped into the gym world, igniting a fervor for intense weight training.

Ever since, I've been captivated by the process of transformation, both physically and mentally. I firmly believe in the potential within each of us to evolve into our best selves. My mission is to inspire and motivate others, showing them that remarkable change is achievable.

Starting out without a coach, I relied heavily on self-education, devouring countless YouTube videos. Along the way, I encountered setbacks and injuries, which fueled my determination to delve deeper into fitness research. Over the past seven years, I've dedicated myself to studying optimal workout techniques and nutrition strategies, supplemented by coursework in nutrition and fitness during my college years.

I did not have a trainer when I started, so I spent a lot of time watching YouTube videos. I have had many injuries in the process, so I have spent the last 5 years researching fitness, and better ways to work out and eat. I also took some nutrition, and fitness classes in College.

Let me help you save you all that time, and injuries by sharing my knowledge with you, so that you can start your fitness journey.

Programs

Online Training

My online program is meticulously crafted to accommodate your workouts from any corner of the globe, provided you have access to a gym facility.

Upon enrollment, I'll arrange a Zoom call with you to delve into your goals, enabling me to tailor a personalized plan designed exclusively for you.

I'll provide you with weekly workout plans and touch base with you weekly to ensure you're on track. Plus, you'll gain access to my exclusive group chat, where you can share your progress with fellow members, forge connections, and build a supportive community.

Go ahead and sign up now, so that we can start with your transformation to become the best version of yourself!

In Person

Thinking about going to the gym, but you feel intimidated because you do not really know what you are doing?

Do not worry! Book an in-person session with me, and I'll be right there by your side throughout your entire workout. From guiding you through each exercise step-by-step to keeping track of your reps and rest intervals, I'll provide hands-on support every step of the way.

Reach out to me, and we'll schedule a one-on-one meeting to discuss your objectives and see if this program is for you.

Given my commitments to college, I'm only able to accommodate a limited number of in-person clients. Secure your spot before availability runs out!
